About Me

My name is Jeffrey Neal Crump. I grew up in Turkey, Texas which in located in the Texas Panhandle. I am the middle child. I have an older sister and a younger brother. I grew up wanting to be a professional football player. I played football in high school but I blew out my knee when I was a senior and that was the end of my football career. I have been working in Information Technology (IT) for more than 20 years. I started working in IT when you had a dumb terminal and you made data requests to a mainframe. I saw the advent of the client-server architecture and the introduction of server technology. I have also witnessed a return to the dumb terminal-mainframe architecture with the explosion of cell phones and small portable devices. I am taking this coding boot camp because I want to work as a developer. I want to create code and services and websites that customers can use for business, social media, or pleasure.

I am hoping this class will provide me the opportunity to become a front-end developer. I hope the experience I gain from this boot camp will allow me to build websites, work as a javascript developer, or work as a beck-end developer.
